# Gasty App Product Definition

## Initial Concept
The Gasty App is a personal finance or expense management application with a monorepo architecture, consisting of a backend implemented in TypeScript using Node.js with Express.js and Hexagonal Architecture, utilizing MongoDB for data persistence. The frontend is built with Next.js (React) and TypeScript, styled with Tailwind CSS.

## Target Audience
The primary users for the Gasty App include:
- **Individual Users:** Individuals who want to manage their personal finances, track expenses, and monitor their budget.
- **Small Business Owners:** Small business owners who need to track business expenses, manage budgets, and generate financial reports.

## Core Functionalities
The Gasty App will provide the following core functionalities:
- **Expense Tracking:** Enabling users to manually input and categorize their daily expenditures and transactions.
- **Budgeting:** Providing tools for users to create, monitor, and manage financial budgets.

## Key Differentiators
The Gasty App will stand out with:
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Simplifying financial data entry with an intuitive UI/UX for all users.
