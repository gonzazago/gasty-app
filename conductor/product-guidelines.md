# Gasty App Product Guidelines

## Tone and Voice
The overall tone and voice for the Gasty App's communications and in-app text should be **Friendly & Approachable**. This approach aims to make financial management less intimidating, fostering a welcoming and easy-to-understand experience for users.

## Visual Identity and Design Principles
The Gasty App's interface will be guided by **Clean & Modern** visual identity and design principles. This emphasizes a minimalist aesthetic, focusing on usability, clarity, and an uncluttered presentation of financial information.
